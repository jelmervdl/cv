Profile

I'm a Research Software Engineer with a background in Artificial Intelligence and over 15 years of experience with developing software for web, desktop, cloud and compute clusters. I'm most comfortable on the bleeding edge, between research and productisation.

I want to make stuff, and I want to continue getting better at it. I really enjoy programming projects where no obvious off-the-shelf solutions are available and where disciplines are combined. Those that introduce me to new fields, concepts and insights. Preferably in a small team, although I'm very comfortable figuring things out on my own.
